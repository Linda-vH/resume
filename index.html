Objective

Hi, I'm Linda. For a long time, I wasn't quite sure which career path suited me. Fortunately, I happened to end up in a position that allowed me to branch out, experiment, and try out a lot of different disciplines. When I was forced to learn some coding to change the appearance of the company's knowledge base, maintain the website, and alter forms created with an online form builder, I realized that coding makes me happy. I like the puzzle, I like the challenge, and most importantly: I love it when it all comes together and that thing I never thought was going to work is looking at me from my screen. Therefore, I decided coding was the way to go. I enrolled in a full stack coding course and am currently working hard on honing my skills so that hopefully, I will be able to call myself a web developer soon.
